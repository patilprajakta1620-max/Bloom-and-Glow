How To Use Our Beauty Products

Simple steps to get the best results from each product

Cleanser

How to Use:
Start your routine by applying a small amount of cleanser on damp skin. Gently massage in circular motions to remove dirt, oil, and makeup. Focus on the T-zone and rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water. Use twice daily for fresh, clean, and balanced skin.

Ingredients: Aloe Vera, Glycerin, Vitamin E, Mild Surfactants

Toner

How to Use:
Apply toner after cleansing using a cotton pad or spray directly on face. It helps tighten pores and restore skin‚Äôs natural pH balance. Allow it to absorb completely before applying serum or moisturizer. Suitable for daily use, morning and night.

Ingredients: Rose Water, Witch Hazel, Niacinamide

Serum

How to Use:
Apply a few drops of serum on clean, dry skin after toner. Gently press and massage into the face and neck until fully absorbed. Serum penetrates deeper to target concerns like dullness, dryness, or uneven skin tone. Use daily, preferably at night, for best results. Follow with moisturizer to lock in hydration.

Ingredients: Hyaluronic Acid, Niacinamide, Vitamin C, Peptides, Botanical Extracts

Moisturizer

How to Use:
Take a small amount and apply evenly on face and neck. Massage gently using upward strokes until fully absorbed. Keeps skin hydrated, soft, and nourished throughout the day. Use daily for healthy and glowing skin.

Ingredients: Shea Butter, Hyaluronic Acid, Vitamin B5

Sunscreen

How to Use:
Apply sunscreen generously on face and exposed areas. Use at least 15 minutes before stepping out in the sun. Reapply every 3‚Äì4 hours for continuous protection. Prevents sun damage, tanning, and premature aging.

Ingredients: Zinc Oxide, Vitamin C, UV Filters

Eye Cream

How to Use:
Dab a small amount around the under-eye area. Use ring finger to gently pat without rubbing. Helps reduce dark circles, puffiness, and fine lines. Use morning and night for best results.

Ingredients: Caffeine, Peptides, Vitamin K

Foundation

How to Use:
Apply small dots on face and blend evenly using sponge or brush. Start from center and move outward for natural coverage. Builds smooth base while evening skin tone. Suitable for daily or party makeup.

Ingredients: Mineral Pigments, Vitamin E

Compact Powder

How to Use:
Apply compact powder using a puff or brush after foundation or moisturizer. Gently press onto face to set makeup and reduce shine. Helps create a smooth, matte finish that lasts longer. Ideal for touch-ups throughout the day. Suitable for all skin types.

Ingredients: Talc, Silica, Zinc Oxide, Natural Pigments, Vitamin E

Concealer

How to Use:
Apply on dark circles, blemishes, or pigmentation. Gently blend using fingertips or brush. Provides targeted coverage without heavy feel. Use after foundation for flawless finish.

Ingredients: Jojoba Oil, Natural Pigments

Blush

How to Use:
Smile and lightly apply on the apples of cheeks. Blend outward for a natural flush. Adds freshness and healthy glow to face. Suitable for everyday and party looks.

Ingredients: Natural Color Pigments

Highlighter

How to Use:
Apply on cheekbones, nose bridge, and cupid‚Äôs bow. Blend softly for luminous glow. Enhances facial features with radiant finish. Use lightly for natural look.

Ingredients: Mica, Vitamin E

Eyeshadow

How to Use:
Apply base shade all over eyelid. Add darker shade on crease and blend well. Creates depth and enhances eye shape. Suitable for day and night looks.

Ingredients: Natural Pigments, Minerals

Eyeliner

How to Use:
Draw a thin line along the upper lash line. Extend slightly for winged look if desired. Defines eyes and enhances lash appearance. Long-lasting and smudge-free.

Ingredients: Carbon Black, Wax Base

Mascara

How to Use:
Apply from root to tip in zig-zag motion. Use multiple coats for volume and length. Enhances lashes without clumping. Perfect for daily and party makeup.

Ingredients: Beeswax, Natural Oils

Lipstick

How to Use:
Apply evenly from center to corners of lips. Use lip liner for defined shape if needed. Provides rich color with smooth finish. Keeps lips soft and moisturized.

Ingredients: Shea Butter, Color Pigments

Lip Balm

How to Use:
Apply lip balm evenly on clean lips. Use throughout the day to keep lips soft and moisturized. Can be applied before lipstick for smooth application. Helps heal dryness and prevent chapped lips. Safe for daily and overnight use.

Ingredients: Beeswax, Shea Butter, Coconut Oil, Vitamin E, Natural Oils

Lotion

How to Use:
Apply generously on clean body skin. Massage gently until absorbed. Keeps skin soft, hydrated, and nourished. Ideal for daily use after shower.

Ingredients: Aloe Vera, Vitamin E

Nail Paint

How to Use:
Apply thin coat on clean nails. Allow first coat to dry before second coat. Provides smooth, glossy, long-lasting finish. Avoid contact until fully dry.

Ingredients: Color Pigments, Resin Base

Perfumes

How to Use:
Spray perfume on pulse points such as wrists, neck, and behind the ears. Apply on clean, dry skin for longer-lasting fragrance. Avoid rubbing after application to preserve the scent notes. Use lightly for daily wear or layer for special occasions. Reapply as needed for a refreshing feel.

Ingredients: Fragrance Oils, Alcohol Denat., Aqua, Essential Oil Blends

Makeup Brushes

How to Use:
Use makeup brushes to apply and blend products smoothly on the face and eyes. Choose the right brush for each product, such as foundation, powder, blush, or eyeshadow. Apply product in gentle strokes or circular motions for a flawless finish. Clean brushes regularly to avoid product buildup and skin irritation. Well-maintained brushes ensure better makeup results and hygiene.

Ingredients: Synthetic Bristles, Natural Fiber Blend, Wooden or Plastic Handle, Aluminum Ferrule
